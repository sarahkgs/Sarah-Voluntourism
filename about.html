Potential Problems

Lack of skills: For example, building homes when you don't have the necessary construction skills will only lead to the locals having to rebuild it.

Impacts children's attachment styles: Volunteers often come for short-term, spend time with children, and will leave.

Damage to local economy: Volunteers perform free work, which can take away opportunities from local people.

When should you engage in voluntourism?

You have specialised skills and training that will allow you to benefit the community (medical skills, construction skills, etc.) If you are a high school student doing voluntourism for your CV, you are less likely to possess these skills.

You can volunteer for an extended period of time - this will ensure that you can see your project through and ensure that you have a positive impact.
